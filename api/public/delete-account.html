Delete Your SocietyOS Account

At SocietyOS, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated information, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at devesociety@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the following information:
    • Full Name
    • Registered Email Address
    • Society Name
  4. Our team will verify your identity and process your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User Account
  • Profile Information
  • Society Membership
  • Resident Information
  • Maintenance Records linked to your account
  • Complaints
  • Notices created by your account

Data Retention

Your account and associated personal data will normally be deleted within 30 days after verification of your request unless a longer retention period is required by law.
